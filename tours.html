Eco Tours

South Florida is home to the largest mangrove forest in North America. These mangrove estuaries are refuges for herons, egrets, stilts, ducks, wood storks, spoonbills, alligators and a gamut of fishes. At points where passages narrow, the mangrove forest grows to form a foliage tunnel. The sunlight shimmering through the canopy mixed with the stillness of the forest vegetation make for a surreal and mystical experience.

Florida is home to the third largest barrier reef system in the world. Hop on a kayak and explore your own piece of this incredibly diverse and beautiful ecosystem. Getting there is half the fun! Participants will traverse over lush sea grass beds and through mangrove islands on their way to the destination. Expect to see an array of marine life including colorful reef fish, dolphin, nurse sharks, spiny lobster, rays, and much more.
