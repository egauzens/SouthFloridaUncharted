Kayak Fishing Trips

The tropical waters of South Florida harbor some of the most sought after inshore gamefish in the world. Anglers can target Snook, Tarpon, Bonefish, Sea Trout, Permit, Jacks, and Snappers while being surrounded by the natural beauty of South Florida. During these fishing forays it is common to cross paths with wildlife like manatees, dolphins, spotted eagle rays, and even the elusive American Crocodile.

South Florida’s warm tropical climate creates the perfect habitat for various exotic species of freshwater fish. Anglers can target everything from Cichlids to Snakeheads and Alligator Gar to Large Mouth Bass. South Florida is also the only place outside of South America where anglers can catch trophy Peacock Bass.

South-East Florida’s close proximity to deep water and the warm Gulf Stream current creates the perfect conditions for kayak anglers to target pelagic fish just a short distance from the beach. On our offshore kayak trips we regularly target prized gamefish like Sailfish, Tuna, Mahi Mahi, Kingfish, Snapper, and Grouper.
