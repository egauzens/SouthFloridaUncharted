About Us

Our passion is the wild side of South Florida. Whether its kayak fishing the deep blue waters in search of sailfish, exploring a tropical hardwood hammock in the Everglades, or traversing the emerald waters of Biscayne Bay on a stand up paddleboard, it is our pleasure to introduce others to the natural playground of South Florida. We offer an array of guided adventures for groups large and small designed to immerse participants in the unique tropical environment that thrives in our region. Forget the high rises of South Beach prepare to explore the coral reefs, mangrove forest, and cypress domes that lie just beyond the city lights. Join us on an unforgettable expedition and discover your own paradise.

Owner/Operator

Meet Dave

David Gauzens was born and raised in Miami Florida. He grew up fishing the numerous waterways of South Florida, biking to lakes and canals to target peacock and largemouth bass. He bought his first kayak at a garage sale for $70 when he was 12 years old with money he earned selling mangos from the backyard. In 2010 David began guiding kayak fishing trips and interpretive Eco tours for the Miami-Dade Parks department.

Through years of experience on the waters of South Florida David has honed his techniques for successfully targeting the most sought after gamefish in South Florida. As an avid outdoorsmen David has a profound passion for the environment and along with fishing enjoys camping, diving, and kiteboarding.

Meet Kev

An avid angler for life, Kevin Hughes has been a dedicated kayak fisherman for the better part of a decade. Originally cutting his teeth on the rivers and lakes of central Ohio, this Midwest native took every opportunity he could to travel and fish in the saltier areas of the country. He developed a passion for the tremendous fishery in south Florida, particularly the incredible and diverse inshore scene. When the opportunity arose to relocate full time to the crystal clear waters of Miami, he jumped at it.

In the years since, he’s become dedicated to the craft of figuring out everything there is to know about fishing here from a kayak. While his specialty is inshore sight fishing the flats and mangroves, he’s embraced all the forms of fishing south Florida offers. From freshwater peacock bass in the canals, to stalking the flats of Biscayne bay for tarpon and bonefish, all the way to fighting tuna and sailfish offshore. He’s spent countless hours on the water learning them all.
